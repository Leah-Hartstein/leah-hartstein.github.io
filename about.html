About Me

Hi! I'm Leah. I'm a recent graduate of a Batchelor of Design at the University of Sydney, majoring in Interaction Design.

My degree taught me a whole bunch of different stuff, but I'm most interested in UI and Web Design. A lot of designers have lofty ideas about changing the world with their products, but I don't. I just love the challenge and thought involved in guiding users through an interface and slightly improving a real product for real, ordinary users.

In my spare time, I like to make dance music and rap beats in my little home studio. For the last thirteen years I have been producing music electronically, creating Dance, Hiphop and Grime music as well as performing as a DJ at live performances and on radio sets. As of 2025, I've released nine solo EPs, five EPs with collaborators as well as 10+ singles, released on labels in the UK, USA and Australia. My productions have been played on the BBC, Apple Beats Radio, as well as FBi in Sydney. I haven't blown up, yet, but I've made an amazing group of friends across the world that have pushed me to try harder, learn more about myself, and develop as an artist.

Other than that - I'm trans! It's pretty great.
